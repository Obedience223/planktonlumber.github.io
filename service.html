_OUR_

Services

Plankton Lumber Services is a reputable lumber company founded in 2018 by CEO Klein Cody, with site operator Raphael Bubble. Over the years, the company has established itself as a leading provider of high-quality lumber products and services. Plankton Lumber Services prides itself on delivering exceptional customer experiences while prioritizing sustainability and responsible forestry practices.

_Below are list of services we offer_

_Skidding Operation_

_Skidding Operation_

Plankton Lumber Services specializes in efficient skidding operations, which involve the careful removal of felled trees from harvesting sites. The company employs skilled professionals and utilizes advanced equipment to transport logs with precision and minimal impact on the surrounding environment. With a focus on sustainability, Plankton Lumber Services ensures responsible skidding practices that prioritize the preservation of forests and ecosystems.

_Logging Operation_

_Logging Operation_

At Plankton Lumber Services, logging operations are conducted with expertise and adherence to industry best practices. Experienced loggers employ advanced methods and equipment to harvest trees efficiently and safely. From tree felling to delimbing and log cutting, the company follows sustainable forestry practices to maintain the long-term health and regeneration of forest resources.

_Fabrication_

_Fabrication_

Plankton Lumber Services excels in transforming raw lumber into value-added products through advanced fabrication techniques. Skilled craftsmen utilize cutting-edge machinery to meet precise specifications. The fabrication process includes activities such as dimensional cutting, shaping, planning, sanding, and joinery. Plankton Lumber Services ensures the highest quality in their fabricated lumber products, catering to the unique needs of customers.

In our fabrication process, Plankton Lumber Services combines skilled craftsmanship with advanced machinery to transform raw lumber into customized products. Each piece undergoes thorough quality control inspections to meet exact specifications and customer expectations.

_Hauling_

_Hauling_

Plankton Lumber Services offers reliable hauling solutions to transport lumber and wood products to their intended destinations. The company maintains a well-maintained fleet of trucks and employs experienced drivers who ensure efficient and timely delivery of materials. Whether it's transporting raw logs from the harvest site or delivering finished lumber products to customers or distribution centers, Plankton Lumber Services prioritizes safety, reliability, and professionalism..

With our hauling services, Plankton Lumber Services ensures the safe and timely delivery of materials, utilizing a well-maintained fleet and experienced drivers who prioritize professionalism and reliability.

_Structural and Architectural_

_Structural and Architectural_

Plankton Lumber Services provides top-notch lumber solutions for both structural and architectural applications. Their structural lumber is meticulously graded and designed to meet engineering requirements for load-bearing components in construction projects. Additionally, their architectural lumber offers exquisite aesthetics for flooring, paneling, moldings, and other decorative features, enhancing the beauty and appeal of architectural designs.

Throughout all their operations, Plankton Lumber Services upholds sustainable practices, adheres to industry regulations, and continuously invests in training, technology, and sustainable forestry management techniques. They strive to improve efficiency, reduce waste, and contribute to the long-term health of forests and ecosystems.

Plankton Lumber Services has earned a stellar reputation for their commitment to quality, sustainability, and exceptional customer service. Their comprehensive range of lumber services caters to diverse industry needs while contributing to the growth and development of the construction and manufacturing sectors.
